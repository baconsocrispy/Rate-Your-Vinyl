Inflight Airplane MODEL tracker

This app is created for aviation enthusiast - who, for any reason needs to know how many airplanes of a specific model
are currently flying in the air and their destinations.
This app allows users the ability to look up certain aircraft types and view how many of those models are in the air
and their destination airport and arrival time.

Example for use: An aviation enthusiast, may enjoy watching planes land and take off.
This person may have an interest in a specific plane make and model and may want to track it down for photos,
or to simply watch it land!

This app is useful and authorized for any relatable uses
